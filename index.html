Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

Welcome to ECE Department

Electronics and Communication Engineering Department at SVIT provides quality education, advanced research opportunities, and practical skills in communication systems, embedded systems, VLSI, and signal processing.

About Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at SVIT, Anantapur, is committed to delivering excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. The department offers advanced laboratories, skilled faculty, and modern learning facilities.

Academics

UG Program

B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Laboratories

Communication Lab, DSP Lab, VLSI Lab, Embedded Systems Lab.

Research Areas

IoT, VLSI Design, Signal Processing, Wireless Communication.

Workshops & Seminars

Regular technical events, industrial visits, and training programs.
